# Sample Draft Incident Report

## Incident Summary

On 2026-05-17 at approximately 14:30, Officer Jane Doe responded to a theft report at 100 Main Street. The reporting party, John Smith, stated that a laptop was missing from an office desk.

## People Involved

- Reporting party: John Smith
- Witness: Maria Lopez

## Narrative

The reporting party stated that the laptop was last seen around 12:00. A witness reported seeing an unknown person near the office area around 13:15. Possible security camera footage may be available for review.

## Evidence Or Items

- Missing laptop
- Possible security camera footage

## Actions Taken

- Statement taken from reporting party
- Possible witness identified
- Security footage review requested

## Missing Information Checklist

- Laptop make, model, and serial number
- Estimated value of missing property
- Full witness statement
- Security footage review result
